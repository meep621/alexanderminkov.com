Hey, I am Alex.

 

I am a resourceful product designer graduate with a passion for finding meaningful solutions to complex problems, through user-centered design.

 

My expertise stretches in various fields of design including UX, industrial, communication, UI, and graphic. During my work placement, I have gathered valuable experience developing solutions on ''IF Design award''-winning products for quality. I  have worked in a high paced agile environments based upon Scrum and Kanban frameworks, both in the UK and internationally.

 

On an academic level, I worked on client project briefs in collaboration with some large British manufacturers such as Morphy Richards, Astro Lighting, and Wilkinson, conceptualising ideas and products around established brand guidelines.

 

I have a team-oriented personality accustomed to meeting deadlines and providing project updates through frequent meetings. Responsibility and good working ethics are the attributes that best describe me.
