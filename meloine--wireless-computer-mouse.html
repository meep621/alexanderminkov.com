Meloine- Wireless computer mouse

Module: Design for Manufacturing

The module aims developing skills of understanding and exploring design practice using both practical and theoretical research. In addition to, establishing an exceptional thinking exploring different industrial and manufacturing processes, technologies and materials.

 

 

Wireless computer mouse

Design for Manufacturing

MELOINE was highly inspired by many architectural and automotive objects such as bridges, electric cars and contemporary computer mice. An important factory visit also,provided vital first hand knowledge of injection moulding methods and design necessities.

The wireless computer mouse was the first university project in which I was required to use a CAD software to generate visual recreation of a product.

In this project were applied basic and advanced manufacturing design principles for plastics. Considering rules for processes as wall thickness ratio, strengthening ribs positioning, shrinkage avoidance etc.
